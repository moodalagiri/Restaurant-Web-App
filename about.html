About

  • About Taste-Of-india

  • Come Experience the Authentic Indian Cuisine

    Taste-Of-India is a casual fine dining restaurant, offering eclectic Indian cuisine. We specialize in North/South Indian three course meal, allowing the customer to select any three dishes from the menu at a reasonable price. A la Carte dining is also available. Our food is sourced from top quality seasonal ingredients, featuring a high percentage of local and organic products. Our menu offers a mix of new and signature dishes. We consider Taste-Of-India to be a truly Indian restaurant honoring many different cooking traditions and interpreting them with our unique creative vision.
